01 — The Challenge02 / 07
The pre-clinical bottleneck

Generating candidates is easy. Choosing them isn't.

Generative models now propose novel protein structures far faster than any lab can synthesize and validate them.

  • AI generation outpaces lab synthesis capacity
  • Manufacturing problems surface late, after resources are spent
  • Safety and governance review lags behind the pace of design
SafeProTeam Dalan
02 — The Solution03 / 07
A checkpoint, not a brake

SafePro reviews every design before it reaches the lab.

It scores each candidate for developability and manufacturability, and screens it for biosecurity and toxicity risk — turning a flood of AI designs into a short, defensible shortlist.
