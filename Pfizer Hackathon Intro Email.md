Hello Hackathon Teams,
 
 
 
Welcome to the preliminary round of the Digital Hackathon! Below are the details, requirements, and resources you'll need to prepare and submit your pitch.
 
 
 
Submission Overview
 
Each team will submit a 5-minute video pitch introducing your idea. A finalized demo or working product is not required at this stage — we're evaluating the strength of your concept and how you communicate it.
 
Due date: Friday, June 19th, by 11:59 PM ET
Format: Unlisted YouTube video link, emailed to DigitalHackathonTeam@pfizer.com
Subject line: {Team Name} – Digital Hackathon Pitch Submission
 
 
How to Submit via Unlisted YouTube
 
An unlisted video is only viewable by people who have the direct link — it won't appear in search results or on your channel. Steps:
 
Sign in to YouTube and click Create → Upload video (top-right camera icon).
Select your video file.
On the Details screen, enter your title as {Team Name} – Digital Hackathon Pitch.
Complete the required audience question ("Not made for kids" is appropriate here).
Click through to the Visibility step and select Unlisted (not Private — Private will block our reviewers).
Publish, then copy the share link and paste it into your submission email.
If you don't have a personal YouTube/Google account, any team member can upload from theirs. Please confirm the link is accessible in an incognito/private browser window before sending.
 
 
 
What Your Pitch Should Cover
 
Your proposed solution
Your roadmap to an MVP
How your solution aligns with Pfizer's interests and organization
