02 — The Solution03 / 07
Where SafePro fits

A checkpoint before synthesis.

SafePro reviews each AI-designed candidate and ranks what's worth making.

  • Ranks developability and manufacturability
  • Screens biosecurity by structure, not just sequence
  • Runs between design and synthesis

Decision · logged
